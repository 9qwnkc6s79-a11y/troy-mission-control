# Boundaries Coffee - Marketing Strategy

## Brand Overview
**Business:** Boundaries Coffee - Drive-thru specialty coffee
**Locations:** Little Elm, TX & Prosper, TX
**Handle:** @boundariescoffee

## Brand Voice
- **Tone:** Casual, warm, community-focused
- **NOT:** Overly AI/corporate, stiff, salesy
- **Vibe:** Like texting a friend who happens to make great coffee
- **Use:** Contractions, conversational language, occasional emojis (not overboard)
- **Avoid:** Generic marketing speak, excessive hashtags, robotic phrasing

## Content Pillars
Rotate through these themes:

1. **Product Shots** (30%)
   - Beautiful drink photos
   - Seasonal specials
   - Food items
   - Latte art

2. **Behind-the-Scenes** (20%)
   - Team spotlights
   - Morning prep
   - Coffee sourcing/roasting partners
   - Day-in-the-life

3. **Community** (25%)
   - Customer features / UGC reposts
   - Local events
   - Storytime events
   - Supporting local

4. **Promos & Announcements** (15%)
   - New menu items
   - Special offers
   - Location news (Prosper opening)
   - Hours/holiday updates

5. **Lifestyle/Relatable** (10%)
   - Monday motivation
   - Coffee humor
   - Relatable moments
   - Seasonal vibes

## Posting Schedule
- **Frequency:** 4 posts per week
- **Days:** Mon, Wed, Fri, Sat (flexible)
- **Best times:** 7-9am (morning coffee crowd), 12-1pm (lunch break)

## Content Calendar Process
1. **Sunday:** Draft week's content + schedule in Later
2. **Daily:** Check comments, respond, repost stories
3. **Friday:** Review week's analytics
4. **Monthly:** Full performance report + strategy adjustments

## Hashtag Strategy
Keep it minimal and relevant:
- #boundariescoffee (branded)
- #littleelmtx #prospertx (local)
- #texascoffee #dfwcoffee (regional)
- 1-2 contextual (e.g., #latteart, #mondaymotivation)

Max 5-8 hashtags, placed in first comment not caption.

## Story Strategy
- Repost customer tags within 24 hours
- Behind-the-scenes glimpses
- Polls/questions for engagement
- Promo reminders

## Google Business Profile
- Post 2x/week (can mirror Instagram content)
- Respond to all reviews within 48 hours
- Keep hours/info updated
- Share events and offers

## Analytics to Track
- Engagement rate (likes + comments / followers)
- Reach and impressions
- Follower growth
- Best performing content types
- Story views and interactions
- Website clicks
