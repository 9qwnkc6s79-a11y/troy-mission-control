Yes! We offer oat milk and other alternative milks including almond milk.

**8. Does Boundaries Coffee have sugar-free options?**
Absolutely. We have sugar-free syrups available for any drink.

Our Coconut Vanilla Latte and nitro cold brew are customer favorites.

Yes! We serve breakfast tacos, kolaches, and other grab-and-go items.

**11. Does Boundaries Coffee have matcha?**
Yes, we have delicious matcha lattes and matcha-based drinks.

**12. Can I get iced coffee at Boundaries Coffee?**
Yes, almost any drink can be made iced. We also have dedicated cold options like cold brew and nitro.

**13. Is Boundaries Coffee locally owned?**
Yes! Boundaries Coffee is locally owned and operated in the DFW area.

Yes! We sell our Cold Brew Concentrate online and in-store.

**19. What makes Boundaries Coffee different?**
We focus on specialty-grade coffee, expertly crafted drinks, and fast, friendly drive-through service.

**20. Are Boundaries Coffee baristas trained?**
Yes, all our baristas go through comprehensive training to ensure quality and consistency.
