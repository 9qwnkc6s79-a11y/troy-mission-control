### ‚ö†Ô∏è IMPORTANT MARKET CONTEXT
The transdermal supplement patch industry is **very different** from traditional supplement manufacturing:

1. **Most patch companies are brands, not contract manufacturers** ‚Äî PatchMD, PatchAid, Nutri-Patch, The Patch Brand all sell their own branded products
2. **True transdermal patch OEM/contract manufacturing is highly specialized** ‚Äî fewer players, higher minimums
3. **FDA classification matters** ‚Äî supplement patches are classified as **cosmetics** (not dietary supplements), which changes labeling/claims requirements
4. **Efficacy claims are limited** ‚Äî you cannot claim transdermal vitamin absorption under current FDA rules for cosmetic products

### What Equipment Is Needed?

**Core Equipment for DNA Methylation Arrays:**
1. **Illumina iScan System** ‚Äî ~$150,000-250,000 (reads the BeadChip arrays)
2. **Illumina Infinium MethylationEPIC v2.0 Kits** ‚Äî pricing requires Illumina account login
   - Available in 8, 16, 32, and 96 sample configurations
   - Requires 250 ng DNA input
   - Bisulfite conversion kits purchased separately
   - **Estimated cost: $200-350 per sample** (array + reagents)
3. **Bisulfite conversion kit** (e.g., Zymo Research EZ DNA Methylation Kit) ‚Äî ~$5-15 per sample
4. **DNA extraction equipment** ‚Äî standard molecular biology lab setup
5. **Bioinformatics pipeline** ‚Äî software to calculate epigenetic clocks (GrimAge, DunedinPACE, PhenoAge, etc.)
6. **CLIA certification** ‚Äî required if processing clinical samples in the US

**Total Equipment Investment:** $200,000-350,000+ for in-house capability

### Can Daniel's Existing Lab Run These?
**Likely not without significant investment.** Running Illumina arrays requires:
- Illumina iScan or NextSeq system
- Trained technicians experienced with methylation arrays
- CLIA certification for clinical testing
- Quality management system
- Bioinformatics team for data analysis and clock calculation

**Recommendation: Partner with an existing CLIA-certified lab rather than building in-house.** This saves $300K+ in equipment and 12+ months in CLIA certification.

---

### What Does a Test Kit Include?
A direct-to-consumer epigenetic age test kit typically includes:
1. **Blood collection device** ‚Äî finger-prick lancet + blood collection card (TruDiagnostic uses this) OR
2. **Saliva collection tube** ‚Äî easier for consumer, but blood generally preferred for methylation
3. **Collection instructions** ‚Äî step-by-step guide with images
4. **Prepaid return mailer** ‚Äî FedEx or USPS prepaid envelope/box
5. **Biohazard bag** ‚Äî for the sample
6. **Unique barcode/activation card** ‚Äî links physical kit to digital account
7. **Consent form** ‚Äî required for processing
8. **Branded packaging** ‚Äî outer box with your brand

**Cost per kit (physical components only):** $8-15 per kit (lancet, tube, mailer, packaging)
